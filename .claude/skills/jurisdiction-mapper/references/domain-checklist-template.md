# Domain Checklist Template

Use this structure for each selected jurisdiction.

```json
{
  "jurisdiction": "South Korea",
  "code": "kr",
  "levels": ["national"],
  "domains": [
    {
      "id": 1,
      "name": "Corporate & commercial transactions",
      "relevance": "high|medium|low",
      "rationale": "One-line rationale for why this domain applies.",
      "priority_primary_sources": [
        "statute text",
        "regulator guideline",
        "enforcement notice"
      ]
    }
  ],
  "uncertain_domains": []
}
```

Ten domains:
1. Corporate & commercial transactions
2. Contract law & obligations
3. Employment & labor law
4. Intellectual property (patents, trademarks, copyright, trade secrets)
5. Privacy & data protection
6. Regulatory & administrative compliance
7. Competition / antitrust
8. Financial services & securities
9. Real property & construction
10. Litigation & dispute resolution
